About Us

Waste Wise Welo Youth Group is a community-based organization committed to environmental conservation and waste management in Kisumu, Kenya. Our primary focus is on cleaning market areas and the Nyalenda slum to create healthier living conditions for residents.

Founded by passionate local youth, our group organizes regular cleanup exercises, waste segregation initiatives, and environmental education programs to empower community members with knowledge about proper waste disposal and recycling.

Through our efforts, we aim to reduce pollution, prevent diseases, and create a cleaner, more sustainable environment for current and future generations.
